Vision

ArtisanXR envisions a world where the rich tapestry of intangible cultural heritage is at everyone's fingertips, transformed by XR and AI technologies into a living, breathing chronicle tailored to each learner. Our vision is a future where every individual's cultural exploration is as unique as their fingerprint, enriched by personalized narratives and interactive experiences that adapt in real-time, ensuring that the flame of global heritage burns brighter with the fuel of modern technology.

Methods

ArtisanXR addresses the challenge of engaging with Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) beyond traditional, passive methods that often fail to captivate or educate effectively. Conventional approaches in museums or classrooms lack interactivity and fail to reflect the dynamic nature of ICH, leading to reduced awareness and appreciation. Our solution employs Multi-Modal Data Analysis (MMDA) and AI to create personalized, immersive game-play experiences, fostering active learning through serious games and interactive storytelling. By integrating Q&A sessions, interaction data, eye-tracking, body motion, and hand tracking, we tailor the learning to each individual's pace and style. ArtisanXR leverages AI and XR to make learning ICH not only educational but also enjoyable, providing a space for users to reflect on and discuss cultural identity. The AI amplifies this personalized journey, offering a gateway to in-depth historical and cultural insights, ensuring every experience is as enriching as it is entertaining.

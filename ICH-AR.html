Munich Walk-Through (In-Progress)

This proposal outlines an AR-based personalized food tour of Munich. Users, using their smart phone, complete a questionnaire to tailor tours to their interests and knowledge. The app, developed in Unity, creates routes and overlays AR directions and elements in real-time. Informative texts about landmarks, generated by LLM, adjust to the user's familiarity with the city. Post-tour feedback assesses knowledge gained, with AR guiding any deviations back on track, ensuring an immersive and educational experience.

Keywords: AR, Munich, City Walk, LLM
